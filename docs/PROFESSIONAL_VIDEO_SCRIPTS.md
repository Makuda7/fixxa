## Video 2: How to Register & Get Started (5-6 minutes)

### Purpose
Step-by-step guide to creating an account and completing a professional profile.

### Script

#### Opening (0:00 - 0:20)
> "Welcome back! Ready to join Fixxa and start getting bookings? In this video, I'll walk you through the entire registration process step by step.
>
> By the end, you'll have a complete professional profile that clients can find and book."

#### Step 1: Create Your Account (0:20 - 1:00)
> "First, go to **www.fixxa.co.za** and click **'Join as a Professional'** in the top right corner.
>
> You'll need to provide:
> - Your full name
> - A valid email address (you'll receive booking notifications here)
> - Your phone number
> - A strong password
>
> Click 'Register' and you'll receive a verification email. Check your inbox and click the link to verify your account."

#### Step 2: Complete Your Professional Profile (1:00 - 3:00)
> "Once verified, log in and you'll see your professional dashboard. Click on 'Edit Profile' to complete your information.
>
> **Basic Information:**
> - Add a professional profile photo (clients trust profiles with clear photos!)
> - Write a brief bio - tell clients who you are and what you do best
> - Add your business name if you have one
>
> **Location Details (IMPORTANT!):**
> - **Province** - Select your province (e.g., Gauteng)
> - **Primary Suburb** - This is your MAIN service area where clients will find you first (e.g., Benoni)
>   * This is super important - clients search by suburb
>   * Make sure spelling is correct - our admin team will verify it
> - **Secondary Areas** - Add other suburbs you're willing to travel to
>   * These show on your profile but you're found by primary suburb first
>
> **Skills and Specialties:**
> - Select your main skill category (Plumbing, Electrical, etc.)
> - Add specific specialties (e.g., 'Geyser Installation', 'Fault Finding')
> - Be specific - this helps clients find exactly what they need
>
> **Pricing (Optional):**
> - Add your hourly rate or call-out fee
> - This gives clients an idea of your pricing
> - You'll still send custom quotes for each job"

#### Step 3: Upload Certifications (3:00 - 4:00)
> "This is what makes you stand out! Upload photos or PDFs of:
> - Trade certificates
> - Licenses (e.g., electrical COC license)
> - Insurance documents
> - Any relevant qualifications
>
> **Why this matters:**
> - Verified professionals get more bookings
> - Clients trust certified professionals
> - You'll get a 'Verified' badge on your profile
>
> To upload:
> 1. Click 'Upload Certification'
> 2. Choose your file (JPEG, PNG, or PDF)
> 3. Add a description (e.g., 'Wireman's License 2024')
> 4. Click 'Upload'
>
> You can upload multiple certificates - the more, the better!"

#### Step 4: Add Photos of Your Work (4:00 - 4:40)
> "Pictures sell! Upload photos of:
> - Completed projects
> - Before and after shots
> - You at work (shows professionalism)
> - Your tools and equipment
>
> Good photos show clients the quality of your work and build trust."

#### Step 5: Submit for Approval (4:40 - 5:30)
> "Once your profile is complete:
> 1. Review all your information
> 2. Make sure your primary suburb is spelled correctly
> 3. Click 'Submit for Approval'
>
> Our admin team will review your profile, verify your certifications, and approve you - usually within 24-48 hours.
>
> You'll receive an email when you're approved, and your profile will go live!
>
> **Pro Tip:** The more complete your profile, the more bookings you'll get. Take time to fill everything out properly.
>
> In the next video, I'll show you how the quote and payment system works. See you there!"

## Video 3: How Quotes & Payments Work (4-5 minutes)

### Purpose
Explain the booking flow, quote system, and payment process.

### Script

#### Opening (0:00 - 0:20)
> "Welcome back! Now that your profile is live, let's talk about how you'll receive bookings, send quotes, and get paid.
>
> Fixxa makes this process simple and professional."

#### Step 1: Receiving Booking Requests (0:20 - 1:00)
> "When a client finds your profile and wants to book you:
>
> 1. You'll receive an **email notification** with the booking details
> 2. Log into your dashboard at **www.fixxa.co.za**
> 3. Go to **'My Bookings'** to see all requests
>
> Each booking shows:
> - Client's name and contact information
> - Service requested
> - Preferred date and time
> - Job description and details
> - Client's location"

#### Step 2: Contacting the Client (1:00 - 1:40)
> "Once you see a booking request:
>
> 1. **Call or WhatsApp the client** using the contact details provided
> 2. Discuss the job details, scope of work, and any special requirements
> 3. Schedule a time that works for both of you
> 4. If needed, arrange a site visit to assess the job
>
> **Important:** Always respond quickly! Clients often reach out to multiple professionals, and the first to respond usually gets the job."

#### Step 3: Sending a Professional Quote (1:40 - 3:20)
> "After discussing the job, send a formal quote through Fixxa:
>
> **How to Send a Quote:**
> 1. In your dashboard, find the booking
> 2. Click **'Send Quote'**
> 3. You'll see the Quote Builder
>
> **Building Your Quote:**
> - Add **line items** - each part of the job with its cost
>   * Example: 'Geyser replacement - R4,500'
>   * Example: 'Copper piping - R800'
>   * Example: 'Labour - R1,200'
> - Click 'Add Line Item' to add more
> - The system automatically calculates the **total**
>
> **Payment Methods:**
> - Select how you accept payment: Cash, EFT, Card
> - If you accept EFT, add your banking details:
>   * Bank name
>   * Account number
>   * Account type
>   * Branch code
>
> **Add Notes:**
> - Include warranty information
> - Mention any terms (e.g., '50% deposit required')
> - Add completion timeline
>
> **Set Validity:**
> - Choose how long the quote is valid (default 7 days)
> - This creates urgency and protects you from price changes
>
> Click **'Send Quote'** and the client receives it via email and can see it in their dashboard."

#### Step 4: Client Accepts Quote (3:20 - 4:00)
> "When the client receives your quote, they can:
> - **Accept** - You'll get a notification and the booking is confirmed
> - **Decline** - They may give a reason, don't take it personally
>
> Once accepted:
> - The booking status changes to 'Confirmed'
> - You can proceed with the job
> - Both you and the client have a record of the agreed price"

#### Step 5: Completing the Job & Getting Paid (4:00 - 4:40)
> "After you complete the work:
>
> 1. Ask the client to **approve completion** in their dashboard
> 2. When they approve, the system automatically generates a **receipt**
>   - Receipt number (e.g., FIX-25-000123)
>   - All line items from your quote
>   - Total amount
>   - Both you and the client receive the receipt via email
>
> **Payment:**
> Currently, clients pay you directly using the payment method you specified (cash, EFT, etc.).
>
> **Future:** We're working on integrated payment processing so you can receive payments directly through the platform!"

#### Closing (4:40 - 5:00)
> "That's it! The quote system keeps everything professional, transparent, and organized.
>
> In the next video, I'll share tips on how to maximize your success on Fixxa and get more bookings. See you there!"

## Video 4: Maximize Your Success on Fixxa (5-6 minutes)

### Purpose
Share best practices, tips, and strategies for getting more bookings and building reputation.

### Script

#### Opening (0:00 - 0:20)
> "Welcome to the final video! You're now set up on Fixxa - but how do you stand out and get more bookings than other professionals?
>
> In this video, I'll share proven tips to maximize your success on the platform."

#### Tip 1: Complete Your Profile 100% (0:20 - 1:00)
> "**Profiles with all sections completed get 3x more bookings.**
>
> Make sure you have:
> - ✅ Professional profile photo (smile, look approachable!)
> - ✅ Detailed bio (tell your story, years of experience)
> - ✅ All certifications uploaded
> - ✅ At least 5-10 photos of your work
> - ✅ Skills and specialties listed
> - ✅ Pricing information
> - ✅ Primary suburb and secondary areas
>
> Clients trust complete profiles - it shows you're serious and professional."

#### Tip 2: Get Verified ASAP (1:00 - 1:30)
> "The 'Verified' badge is your secret weapon.
>
> **How to get verified:**
> - Upload clear photos of your certifications
> - Make sure they're current and legible
> - Include licenses, insurance, trade certificates
>
> **Why it matters:**
> - Verified professionals appear higher in search results
> - Clients filter by 'Verified Only'
> - Builds immediate trust
> - You'll get more quote requests"

#### Tip 3: Respond Within 30 Minutes (1:30 - 2:10)
> "**Speed matters!** Clients often contact multiple professionals, and the first to respond usually gets the job.
>
> **Best practices:**
> - Turn on **email notifications** for new bookings
> - Check your dashboard multiple times per day
> - Respond within 30 minutes if possible
> - Even if you're busy, send a quick message: 'Got your request, will call you within the hour'
>
> **Pro Tip:** Download the Fixxa app (coming soon!) for instant push notifications."

#### Tip 4: Send Professional, Detailed Quotes (2:10 - 3:00)
> "Your quote is your first impression. Make it count!
>
> **Quote Best Practices:**
> - Break down costs clearly (don't just send one total)
>   * Bad: 'Total: R5,000'
>   * Good: 'Labour: R2,000 | Materials: R2,500 | Transport: R500'
> - Be specific about what's included
> - Add notes about warranty or guarantees
> - Include your payment terms clearly
> - Set a reasonable validity period (7-14 days)
>
> **Pricing Tips:**
> - Don't be the cheapest - compete on value, not price
> - Justify higher prices with quality materials or warranties
> - Be transparent - no hidden fees
>
> **Follow Up:**
> If the client hasn't responded in 2-3 days, send a friendly follow-up message."

#### Tip 5: Build Your Reputation with Reviews (3:00 - 3:40)
> "Reviews are GOLD on Fixxa. Each 5-star review increases your booking rate.
>
> **How to get great reviews:**
> 1. Do excellent work (obvious, but worth saying!)
> 2. After completing a job, politely ask the client to leave a review
> 3. Send a message: 'If you're happy with my work, I'd really appreciate a review on Fixxa'
> 4. Make it easy - tell them exactly where to leave the review
>
> **Respond to Reviews:**
> - Always thank clients for positive reviews
> - If you get a negative review, respond professionally and offer to make it right
> - This shows future clients you care about service"

#### Tip 6: Keep Your Availability Updated (3:40 - 4:10)
> "If you're fully booked or taking time off:
>
> 1. Update your availability status in your dashboard
> 2. This prevents booking requests when you can't take work
> 3. Update it back when you're ready for new jobs
>
> **Benefit:** Clients appreciate honesty, and you won't get overwhelmed with requests you can't fulfill."

#### Tip 7: Expand Your Secondary Areas Strategically (4:10 - 4:40)
> "Remember, clients find you by your PRIMARY suburb, but you can service SECONDARY areas.
>
> **Strategy:**
> - Start with your main area (primary suburb)
> - Once you have good reviews, add 2-3 neighboring suburbs as secondary areas
> - This shows clients you're willing to travel
> - Don't add too many - be realistic about what you can serve well
>
> **Pro Tip:** Update your areas seasonally if your availability changes."

#### Bonus Tips (4:40 - 5:20)
> "Here are a few more quick tips:
>
> **Professionalism:**
> - Always arrive on time (or call if delayed)
> - Wear clean, branded clothing if possible
> - Clean up after yourself - clients notice
> - Be polite and respectful
>
> **Communication:**
> - Use proper grammar in quotes and messages
> - Be clear about timelines and expectations
> - Update clients on progress for bigger jobs
>
> **Safety:**
> - Always follow safety protocols
> - Use proper equipment
> - Get COC certificates where required (electrical, plumbing)
>
> **Growth:**
> - Track which services get the most bookings
> - Consider adding related skills (e.g., plumber adds geyser installation)
> - Invest in certifications that make you more valuable"

#### Closing & Encouragement (5:20 - 6:00)
> "You're now fully equipped to succeed on Fixxa!
>
> **Remember:**
> - Complete your profile 100%
> - Get verified
> - Respond quickly
> - Send professional quotes
> - Collect reviews
> - Be professional and reliable
>
> **The professionals who follow these tips are the ones getting 10-20 bookings per month.**
>
> We're excited to have you on Fixxa, and we're here to support your success. If you ever have questions, contact us at **support@fixxa.co.za**.
>
> Now go out there and build your business! Welcome to the Fixxa family."
