# Fixxa Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 13, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Fixxa ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Fixxa mobile application and website.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information You Provide

**Account Information:**
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Password (encrypted)
- Profile picture (optional)

**Professional Information (For Service Providers):**
- Business name
- Service categories
- Certifications and qualifications
- ID verification documents
- Portfolio photos
- Business address

**Booking Information:**
- Service requests
- Booking details
- Service addresses
- Payment information (handled by third-party providers)

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

**Location Data:**
- Approximate location (when you grant permission)
- Used to find nearby professionals
- Used to calculate distances to service providers
- Can be disabled in device settings

**Usage Data:**
- App usage statistics
- Features used
- Time spent in app
- Device information (type, OS version)

**Communications:**
- Messages sent through the app
- Reviews and ratings
- Photos shared in messages

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

### Core Services:
- Create and manage your account
- Connect clients with service professionals
- Facilitate bookings and appointments
- Enable in-app messaging
- Process and manage service requests
- Display reviews and ratings

### Location Services:
- Show professionals near your location
- Calculate distances to service providers
- Improve search results based on proximity

### Safety & Security:
- Verify professional credentials
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Ensure platform safety
- Resolve disputes

### Communication:
- Send booking confirmations
- Notify about booking updates
- Send service reminders
- Respond to support requests
- Send important account notifications

### Improvement:
- Analyze app usage to improve features
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Develop new features
- Optimize user experience

## Information Sharing

We DO NOT sell your personal information. We only share information in these circumstances:

### With Service Professionals:
- Your name and contact information when you book a service
- Service location and requirements
- Communication through our messaging system

### With Clients (For Professionals):
- Professional profile information
- Reviews and ratings
- Portfolio photos
- Service availability

### With Service Providers:
- **Cloudinary:** For image storage and optimization
- **SendGrid:** For email notifications
- **Expo/React Native:** For app infrastructure
- **Railway:** For hosting services

### Legal Requirements:
- When required by law
- To protect rights and safety
- To enforce our terms of service
- In connection with business transfers

## Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
- Secure password storage (bcrypt hashing)
- Session-based authentication
- Virus scanning for file uploads
- Regular security audits
- Secure cloud storage

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control:
- View your personal information in your profile
- Update or correct your information
- Delete your account and associated data
- Download your data (upon request)

### Location Permissions:
- Grant or deny location access
- Change location settings in device settings
- Use app without location (with limited features)

### Communications:
- Opt out of promotional emails
- Cannot opt out of critical service emails
- Manage notification preferences in settings

### Delete Account:
- Contact support@fixxa.co.za
- Account deleted within 30 days
- Some information retained for legal compliance

## Children's Privacy

Fixxa is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover we have collected information from a child, we will delete it immediately.

## Data Retention

We retain your information:
- **Active Accounts:** While your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** 30 days for recovery
- **Legal Requirements:** As required by law
- **Legitimate Business Needs:** For dispute resolution and fraud prevention

## Location Data Specifics

### iOS Location Permission:
**NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription:**
"Fixxa needs your location to find professionals near you and show distances to service providers."

### How We Use Location:
- Find professionals within your area
- Display distance to each professional
- Sort results by proximity
- Improve search relevance

### You Control Location:
- Permission requested when needed
- Can be revoked in device settings
- App works without location (with limited features)
- Never tracked in background
