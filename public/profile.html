Safety & Best Practices

Before booking, please review these important safety tips for a secure experience:

💬

Keep Communication on Platform

Always message through Fixxa. Never share phone numbers, emails, or move conversations off-platform. This protects you and ensures we can help resolve any issues.

💳

Payment Safety Tips

Always ask for a receipt when making payments. When possible, use bank transfer as it is safer and provides a clear payment trail. Avoid cash payments without proper documentation.

🏠

Meet in Safe Locations

For in-person services, ensure someone knows about your appointment. Consider having someone present during the service, especially for home visits.

✅

Check Professional's Profile

Review ratings, reviews, certifications, and verification status before booking. Look for verified professionals with positive feedback.

📸

Use In-App Image Sharing

Share photos of the job through Fixxa messages. This helps professionals provide accurate quotes and creates a record for dispute resolution.

🚩

Report Suspicious Behavior

If a professional asks you to pay outside the platform, share contact info, or behaves inappropriately, report them immediately through the app.
