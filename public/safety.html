Safety & Security Guidelines

At Fixxa, your safety is our priority. While we vet professionals as best as possible, it's important for clients to remain vigilant. Please follow these guidelines to protect yourself and your property:

1. Vetting Professionals

  • Check the professional’s profile, reviews, and ratings before confirming a service.
  • Never share sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary for the service.

2. Payment & Financial Safety

  • Use the Fixxa platform for payments when applicable.
  • Avoid paying outside the platform before services are rendered to prevent fraud.
  • Do not provide credit card or banking information to professionals directly.

3. Personal Safety During Service

  • Prefer having services performed during daylight hours when possible.
  • Ensure someone is present during the service, if applicable.
  • Trust your instincts—if something feels unsafe, pause or cancel the appointment.

4. Reporting Issues

  • Immediately report any suspicious behavior or fraud to Fixxa support.
  • Document incidents with photos, timestamps, or any other evidence.

Disclaimer: While Fixxa strives to vet all professionals, we cannot guarantee the quality of work or safety during services. Users agree to take personal responsibility and follow these guidelines.
