Privacy Policy

Last updated: [October 2025]

At Fixxa ("we", "us" or "our"), protecting your personal information is important. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and the rights you have under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

1. The information we collect

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number, profile photo, and other details you provide when registering as a client or professional.
  • Service details: job descriptions, service requests, location, job history, quotes and reviews.
  • Payment information: where payments are enabled we use third-party payment processors and do not store full payment card details on our systems.
  • Device and usage data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, cookies and analytics data to help us operate and improve the platform.
  • Communications: messages between users and optional support enquiries you send to us.

2. Why we collect your information

We collect and process personal information to:

  • Provide and operate the Fixxa platform and match clients with professionals.
  • Manage accounts, process service requests and (where applicable) payments.
  • Communicate important information, updates and marketing where you have consented.
  • Improve and personalise the service, prevent fraud and protect our users.
  • Meet our legal and regulatory obligations under POPIA and other applicable laws.

3. Lawful basis for processing

Under POPIA, we process personal information where it is necessary for the performance of a contract, where we have legitimate interests (such as preventing fraud and improving the service), or where you have given your consent (for example, marketing messages).

4. How we share and disclose information

  • With professionals: we share relevant client details (name, contact, job description) with professionals to enable service delivery.
  • Service providers: third-party providers who help us run the platform (payment processors, hosting providers, analytics providers). These providers act as processors and are required to protect your data.
  • Legal requirements: we may disclose information where required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • Business transfers: in the event of a merger, acquisition or sale of assets, personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction (with appropriate safeguards).

5. International transfers

Fixxa may use third-party services that store or process personal information outside South Africa. When this happens, we require appropriate safeguards to ensure your data remains protected to POPIA standards.

6. Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate the site and analyse usage. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser, and we will display a cookie notice on first visit.

7. Data security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), access controls, and secure hosting. While we strive to protect your data, no internet transmission is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes set out in this policy, to comply with legal obligations, and to resolve disputes. Where possible we will anonymise data that is no longer required.

9. Your rights

You have rights under POPIA, including the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you;
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information;
  • Request deletion or restriction of processing in certain circumstances;
  • Object to processing where applicable; and
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Information Officer at the details below. We will respond in accordance with applicable law.

10. Children

The Fixxa service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child without consent, we will take steps to delete that information.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of Fixxa after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact and Information Officer

If you have questions, wish to exercise your data rights, or want to make a complaint, please contact:
