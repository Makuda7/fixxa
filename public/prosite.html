💡 FixxaTips: Get the Best Out of Fixxa

🚀 Maximize Your Success

Follow these proven tips to attract more clients, earn better reviews, and grow your business on Fixxa!

🏆 Tip 1: Keep Your Profile Polished

Profiles with clear photos, professional descriptions, and verified badges get 3x more bookings.

Update your profile photo, add a compelling bio describing your skills, upload portfolio images of your best work, and keep your certifications current. A complete profile builds trust!

📍 Tip 2: Choose the Right Suburbs

List areas you actually want to work in — clients prefer local pros who can arrive quickly.

Be specific about your service areas. Clients search by location and choose professionals nearby. Update your primary and secondary service areas to match where you're willing to travel.

⚡ Tip 3: Respond Fast

When job requests come in, speed matters! Clients often pick the first few pros who reply.

Turn on push notifications, check the app regularly, and respond within minutes when possible. A quick "I'm available and interested!" can win you the job before competitors even see it.

💬 Tip 4: Communicate Clearly

Keep messages polite and professional.

Example Message:

"Hi there! Thanks for reaching out. I can assist with that tomorrow morning. Does 9am work for you?"

Be friendly, confirm details, ask questions if anything is unclear, and always follow up. Good communication builds confidence and leads to repeat business.

⭐ Tip 5: Collect Reviews

After each job, kindly ask your client to leave a review.

Positive reviews boost your ranking and make future clients confident to hire you. A simple "If you're happy with my work, I'd really appreciate a review!" goes a long way. Reviews are your best marketing tool.

🔧 Tip 6: Go the Extra Mile

A simple thank-you, clean workspace, or small gesture goes a long way.

Happy clients bring repeat work — and referrals. Clean up after yourself, arrive on time, exceed expectations, and treat every job like it matters. Word-of-mouth is powerful on Fixxa!

🚀 Tip 7: Stay Active

Log in regularly, update your profile, and keep your calendar accurate.

The more active you are, the more Fixxa promotes you in search results. Set your availability correctly, respond to messages promptly, and stay engaged. Active professionals get more visibility and more jobs!

✨ Bonus Tip: Build Your Reputation

Consistency is key! Follow these tips regularly, deliver quality work, and watch your bookings grow. The top-rated professionals on Fixxa didn't get there overnight — they earned it through great service, one job at a time. You can too! 🎯

📜 Platform Rules & Guidelines

⚠️ Important Notice

These rules protect you, our clients, and the integrity of the Fixxa platform. Violations may result in account suspension or permanent removal from the platform. Please read carefully and comply with all guidelines.

🚫 Prohibited: Sharing Contact Information

You MUST NOT share or request:

  • Phone numbers (yours or theirs)
  • Email addresses
  • WhatsApp, Telegram, or other messaging app contacts
  • Social media profiles (Facebook, Instagram, etc.)
  • Physical addresses before booking approval
⚠️ Warning:

All messages are monitored. Sharing contact info or screenshots containing contact details will result in immediate account suspension.

✅ Why? Keeping communication on-platform protects both parties, ensures quality service, and allows us to help resolve disputes.

💳 Payment Guidelines & Best Practices

✅ Accepted Payment Methods:

  • Bank Transfer (EFT) - RECOMMENDED - Safest option with clear payment trail
  • Cash - Only upon job completion and with receipt
  • Mobile payment apps (SnapScan, Zapper, etc.)
⚠️ IMPORTANT - Always Provide Receipts:

You MUST provide a receipt for every payment received. This protects both you and the client. Include: Date, service description, amount paid, and your contact details.

✅ Payment Best Practices:

  • Discuss and agree on pricing BEFORE starting any work
  • For large jobs, a deposit (30-50%) is acceptable
  • Collect final payment only after work is completed to client's satisfaction
  • Keep your own records of all payments received
  • If a client disputes payment, report it through the Fixxa platform
🚫 Never Accept:

Cryptocurrency, gift cards, or suspicious payment methods. If a client requests unusual payment methods, report it to Fixxa support immediately.

✅ Why these guidelines? Following safe payment practices protects your earnings, builds trust with clients, and helps resolve disputes if they occur.

✅ Required: Professional Conduct

You MUST:

  • ✅ Respond to messages within 24 hours
  • ✅ Provide accurate availability and pricing information
  • ✅ Show up on time for confirmed bookings
  • ✅ Complete jobs to the best of your ability
  • ✅ Be respectful and professional at all times
  • ✅ Report any issues or disputes through the platform
  • ✅ Update your profile with accurate qualifications
  • ✅ Upload legitimate certifications and credentials

📅 Booking & Scheduling Guidelines

  • Accept/Decline Promptly: Respond to booking requests within 24 hours
  • No Last-Minute Cancellations: Canceling less than 12 hours before a booking may affect your rating
  • Reschedule Requests: Use the platform's reschedule feature, don't arrange outside
  • Availability Updates: Keep your schedule current to avoid double bookings
  • No-Shows: Failing to show up without notice can result in account suspension

📸 Image & Content Guidelines

Profile Photos & Portfolio:

  • ❌ No screenshots containing contact information
  • ❌ No inappropriate, offensive, or misleading images
  • ✅ Use professional photos of your actual work
  • ✅ Ensure photos are clear and well-lit
  • ✅ Only upload work you personally completed
Note:

Workers cannot send images to clients (security measure). Clients can share job photos with you.

🎓 Verification & Certification Rules

  • No Fake Certificates: Only upload genuine, verifiable credentials
  • Accurate Information: All profile information must be truthful
  • Verification Process: Submit required documents promptly for verification
  • Update Expired Credentials: Keep certifications current
⚠️ Serious Violation:

Submitting fake certifications or credentials will result in immediate permanent ban and potential legal action.

⚖️ Dispute Resolution

If you have a problem with a client or booking:

  • ✅ Contact admin support through the "Contact & Feedback" tab
  • ✅ Provide screenshots of messages (if relevant)
  • ✅ Explain the issue clearly and professionally
  • ❌ Do NOT contact the client outside the platform
  • ❌ Do NOT leave negative public comments
  • ❌ Do NOT retaliate or harass the client

🚨 Consequences of Rule Violations

First Offense:

  • ⚠️ Written warning
  • ⚠️ 7-day account suspension
  • ⚠️ Visibility reduction in search results

Second Offense:

  • 🚫 30-day account suspension
  • 🚫 Loss of verification status
  • 🚫 Pending earnings held for review

Third Offense / Serious Violations:

  • ❌ Permanent account termination
  • ❌ Forfeiture of all pending earnings
  • ❌ Ban from re-registering on Fixxa
  • ❌ Potential legal action (for fraud or serious misconduct)

✅ Acknowledgment

By using the Fixxa platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to comply with all rules and guidelines outlined above. These rules are in place to create a safe, fair, and professional environment for everyone.
