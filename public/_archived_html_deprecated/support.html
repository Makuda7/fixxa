Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We respond to all requests within 48 hours. Urgent safety concerns are prioritized and typically addressed within a few hours during business hours.

What if I have a dispute with a Professional/Client?

Please submit a complaint using the form above with category "Complaint about Service". Include all relevant details, booking ID, and what outcome you're seeking. We'll investigate and mediate where appropriate.

Can Fixxa guarantee refunds for poor service?

Fixxa is a platform connecting clients with independent professionals. We are not responsible for the quality of services performed. However, we will investigate complaints and may remove professionals who consistently provide poor service.

How do I report a safety concern?

Select "Safety Concern" as the category and provide all details. For immediate emergencies, contact local authorities (Police: 10111) first, then report to us.

How do I delete my account and data?

Email us at support@fixxa.co.za with subject "POPIA Data Deletion Request" from your registered email address. We'll process your request within 30 days as required by South African law.
