# 3. SAFETY & SECURITY GUIDELINES

## For Clients:

### Before Booking:
✅ **Verify Credentials**
- Check Professional's ratings and reviews
- Verify certifications displayed on profile
- Review work portfolio if available

✅ **Communicate Clearly**
- Message Professional before booking to discuss requirements
- Confirm availability and pricing
- Ask questions about their experience

✅ **Use Platform Messaging**
- Keep all communication on Fixxa for record-keeping
- Don't share personal contact information initially

### During Service:
✅ **Be Present**
- Be home or available during service delivery
- Supervise work in progress when appropriate
- Secure valuables and personal items

✅ **Trust Your Instincts**
- If something feels unsafe, end the service
- Report any inappropriate behavior immediately
- Use emergency services (10111) for immediate threats

✅ **Payment Safety**
- Use platform payment methods when available
- Get itemized quotes for materials/supplies
- Don't pay full amount before work starts

### After Service:
✅ **Inspect Work**
- Check that work meets agreed standards
- Document issues with photos
- Request fixes for unsatisfactory work before final payment

✅ **Leave Honest Reviews**
- Help other users make informed decisions
- Report serious safety concerns to Fixxa

## For Professionals:

### Safety Practices:
✅ **Screen Clients**
- Review client profile and booking history
- Communicate clearly before accepting bookings
- Decline suspicious or uncomfortable requests

✅ **Protect Personal Information**
- Don't share bank details outside platform
- Be cautious of requests to work "off-platform"
- Report clients asking to circumvent Fixxa

✅ **On-Site Safety**
- Inform someone of your location
- Bring necessary safety equipment
- Leave if situation feels unsafe

✅ **Professional Conduct**
- Arrive on time and in professional attire
- Respect client's property and privacy
- Communicate delays or issues promptly

### Scam Prevention:

🚨 **Red Flags to Watch:**
- Requests to communicate off-platform immediately
- Upfront payment for materials before meeting
- Requests for personal banking information
- Jobs in remote/isolated locations without verification
- Offers that seem too good to be true

## Reporting Issues:

**Report to Fixxa:**
- Email: fixxaapp@gmail.com
- Subject: "Safety Report - Urgent"
- Include: User details, date/time, description, evidence (screenshots, photos)

**Emergency Services:**
- Police: 10111
- Ambulance: 10177
- Fire: 10111

**We Take Reports Seriously:**
- All reports investigated within 24-48 hours
- Users may be suspended pending investigation
- Serious violations result in permanent ban
- Criminal activity reported to authorities

# 5. COOKIE POLICY

### What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device to remember your preferences and improve your experience.

### Cookies We Use:

#### Essential Cookies (Required):
- **Session Cookies:** Keep you logged in
- **Security Cookies:** Prevent fraud and protect your account
- **Load Balancing:** Ensure platform performance

#### Performance Cookies (Optional):
- **Analytics:** Understand how users navigate the platform
- **Error Tracking:** Identify and fix technical issues

#### Functionality Cookies (Optional):
- **Preferences:** Remember your language, location settings
- **Recent Searches:** Improve search experience

### Third-Party Cookies:
- **Payment Processors:** Secure payment processing
- **Google Analytics:** Usage statistics (anonymized)

### Managing Cookies:
You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may limit platform functionality.

**Browser Cookie Settings:**
- Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
- Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
