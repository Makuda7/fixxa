## =¡ Tips for Testing

- **Be realistic:** Use the app as if you really needed the service
- **Try edge cases:** What if you cancel? What if you reschedule twice?
- **Test on mobile:** Most users will be on phones
- **Report everything:** Even small UI issues matter
- **Test email:** Check your spam folder for notifications
